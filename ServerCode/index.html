Play music together.


Create an interactive shared playlist with your friends and watch as songs, movies, and YouTube videos are played perfectly in sync among all devices.



music

Synced connects multiple iOS devices into one perfectly synchronized loudspeaker. Anyone can add songs to the playlist.

All you need is an Internet connection and Bluetooth.

videos

Watch YouTube videos across iOS devices in sync. Or watch a local one while everyone else listens on their own devices in sync.
