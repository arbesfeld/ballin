About

Play Music Together

Synced connects multiple iOS devices into one perfectly synchronized loudspeaker. Everyone can choose songs to be played - all you need is an internet connection and have bluetooth enabled.

Share with Each Other

Create an interactive shared playlist with your friends and watch how songs, movies and YouTube videos are played perfectly in sync between all devices! Share YouTube videos with your friends and watch at the same time on all devices.

Party in a Whole New Way

Flash lights to the beat of the music and party the night away with your friends!
